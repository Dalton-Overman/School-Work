      Swiss subsidiary and will be completed before the end of the year. The Act,
      which was passed in October 2004, provides a temporary incentive for United
      States companies to repatriate accumulated income earned in foreign
      jurisdictions at a reduced income tax cost. The estimated U.S. federal and
