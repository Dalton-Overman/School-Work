<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The purpose of the K-Tron International, Inc. 2006 Equity Compensation Plan (the &#147;Plan&#148;) is to
provide (i)&nbsp;employees of K-Tron International, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) and its subsidiaries and (ii)
non-employee members of the Board of Directors of the Company with the opportunity to receive
grants of incentive stock options, nonqualified stock options, stock appreciation rights, stock
awards, stock units and other stock-based awards. The Company believes that the Plan will
encourage the participants to contribute materially to the growth of the Company, thereby
benefitting the Company&#146;s shareholders, and will align the economic interests of the participants
with those of the shareholders.
</DIV>

</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(b) &#147;Cause&#148; shall mean, except to the extent specified otherwise by the Committee, a finding
by the Committee that the Grantee (i)&nbsp;has breached his or her employment or service contract with
the Employer, (ii)&nbsp;has engaged in disloyalty to the Employer, including, without limitation, fraud,
embezzlement, theft, commission of a felony or proven dishonesty, (iii)&nbsp;has disclosed trade secrets
or confidential information of the Employer to persons not entitled to receive such information,
(iv)&nbsp;has breached any written non-competition, non-solicitation or confidentiality agreement
between the Grantee and the Employer or (v)&nbsp;has engaged in such other behavior detrimental to the
interests of the Employer as the Committee determines.
</DIV>
