executive officers and other employees pursuant to bonus guidelines used in the
preparation of the Company&#8217;s budget for that year.&#160;&#160;These guidelines
create a bonus pool for the entire Company if the budgeted earnings per share
are achieved.&#160;&#160;Under these guidelines, the target bonus for 2007 for

their base salaries.&#160;&#160;Despite these targets, the annual cash incentive
award for any executive officer may be more or less than the applicable target,
depending on the Company&#8217;s financial performance for the year, the Committee&#8217;s

Committee may choose to consider.</font></div>

required to achieve the $4.60 EPS target.&#160;&#160;Using the Company&#8217;s actual

target was $20,827,000.&#160;&#160;The actual pre-tax, pre-bonus income of the
Company in 2007 was $35,438,000, or an excess of $14,611,000 over the amount
needed to achieve the $4.60 EPS target and related bonus pool of
$2,196,950.&#160;&#160;Given this financial performance, the Committee approved
adding 20% of the excess amount, or $2,922,200, to the incentive compensation
pool for 2007.&#160;&#160;As a result, the total incentive compensation pool for
2007 was $5,119,150, or 133% higher than the target pool based upon the
budget.&#160;&#160;The Company&#8217;s diluted earnings per share in 2007, after

2007 budget by 62.8%.</font></div>
